Safety and Security Window Film

Clear Guard is an optically clear protection film for vehicle windshields and homes. Clear Guard protects against cracks and chips on glass. Clear Guard also prolongs forced entry into your home or vehicle. Clear Guard is equipped with heat rejection properties which help maintain indoor temperatures the way you want.

Clear Guard Safety

The standard in protection. Meant to be a sacrificial layer that protects against chips and cracks and keeps your glass from shattering into pieces.

Features

Scratch Resistant

Our films are hard-coated to provide anti-scratch properties, keeping your film impeccable for years to come.

Safety Chip Protection

Our films feature a high-tensile base layer, making them hard to puncture. The nanoGard films hold shattered glass in place, preventing glass going inwards towards the passengers.

High Visibility

Combining a multiplex of features while still displaying crystal clear visibility, this is the nanoGard magic we create behind the scenes.

High Temperature Resistance

High quality UV additives prolong the life of the film on the vehicle, making the film resistant to becoming brittle too fast.

Easy Installation

Good tolerance to warping while heating to conform to shape of substrate, making it extremely easy to handle while installing.

Clear Guard + Safety

New formulation with a tough PTFE coating infused into the layers helps increase the durability of the film while maintaining full visibility.
